About TaskFlow Manager

TaskFlow Manager is a productivity tool designed to streamline task management and improve workflow efficiency. Our intuitive interface allows users to add, edit, and manage tasks within a calendar view, ensuring deadlines and priorities are easily tracked.

The application integrates a simple task creation system that displays tasks on the calendar, offering a clear view of upcoming commitments. Future updates will include AI-powered task suggestions, improved filtering options, and deeper calendar integrations.

Whether you're managing personal tasks, work projects, or academic deadlines, TaskFlow Manager is your go-to solution for effective task management.
